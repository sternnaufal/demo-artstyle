Mood

Unsettling, familiar-yet-wrong, empty — the feeling of being in a place that should have people but doesn't. Nostalgic dread.

Era

2010s–present. Born from internet creepypasta (The Backrooms, 2019) and analog horror. Rooted in 90s architectural photography.

Colors

Sickly beige (#e8e0d0), fluorescent yellow-white, faded browns. The palette of abandoned office buildings and empty malls.

Typography

Inter (sans-serif) — thin, neutral, unremarkable. The ordinariness is the horror. No personality in the letterforms.

Characteristics

Empty corridors, fluorescent lighting, repeating textures, liminal thresholds, forced perspective, eerie familiarity, no people.
