✦ manifesto

beauty in decay

issue no. 7 · 2025

The things that are broken tell the truest stories. A torn poster on a brick wall says more than a perfect billboard. We collect the fragments — the half-erased graffiti, the wrinkled zine, the photograph fading in the sun. Perfection is a lie. Grunge is the truth underneath.

✕
✦ collage

found objects

from the cutting room floor

Ticket stubs. Receipts. Wrappers. Cigarette butts. The detritus of daily life arranged into compositions that would make Duchamp smile. We glue them onto paper with dried glue and let the edges curl. The imperfection is the point.

∅
✦ sound

the 4-track years

recorded in a basement · 1993

Before digital silence, there was hiss. The warmth of tape saturation. The accident of a microphone too close to an amp. Glorious, beautiful noise. We chase that feeling — the moment when the signal breaks and becomes something raw.
