The Uncanny

Surrealism · Dream · Paradox

The False Mirror

A sky reflected in an eye that sees nothing. The boundary between observer and observed dissolves. Who dreams — you, or the dream?

Soft Structures

Weightless elephants. Architecture of jelly. The laws of physics are merely suggestions when the subconscious holds the pen.

Doors Everywhere

Windows that open to brick walls. Doors that lead to the sky. Thresholds that threshold nothing. Passage is a state of mind.

Time Unbound

Clocks that drip like honey. Hours that stretch like taffy. Duration divorced from measurement. The present is a room with no walls.
