★ art for everyone ★

Mood

Bold, playful, ironic — high art meets low culture. Celebrity worship, consumerism, and repetition as commentary.

Era

1950s–1960s UK/US. Key figures: Andy Warhol, Roy Lichtenstein, Jasper Johns, James Rosenquist, Claes Oldenburg.

Colors

Bright primaries — red (#e60000), yellow (#ffde00), blue (#0057b8), black, white. High contrast, Ben-Day dots texture.

Typography

Bold comic-style sans (Anton). Speech bubbles, onomatopoeia (WHAAM!, POP!), bold caps, comic book lettering.

Characteristics

Ben-Day dots, comic panels, celebrity imagery, repetition, appropriation of commercial art, bold outlines, flat color.
