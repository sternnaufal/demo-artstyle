The grid is not a constraint — it is liberation through structure.

Mood

Pure, universal, elemental — reducing art to its absolute essence. Harmony through geometric reduction.

Era

1917–1931. Founded by Theo van Doesburg. Key figure: Piet Mondrian, Gerrit Rietveld. Dutch avant-garde.

Colors

Red (#d4000f), blue (#0033a0), yellow (#ffd700) + black, white, gray. No green, no purple, no curves.

Typography

Inter (sans-serif) — bold geometric uppercase. No serifs, no decoration, no personality beyond pure form.

Characteristics

Straight lines, right angles, primary colors, asymmetric grid, reduction to essentials, horizontal-vertical composition.
