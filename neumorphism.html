What is Neumorphism?

A UI design trend that blends flat design with skeuomorphism. Elements appear to be extruded from the background — embossed or debossed — using subtle shadows and highlights that mimic soft plastic or clay.

Dark mode
Notifications

Mood

Soft, tactile, calm — interfaces that feel tangible and physical. Gentle shadows create a sense of depth without violence.

Era

2019–2021. Popularized by UI designers on Dribbble. Coined by Michał Malewicz. A trend within the broader "soft UI" movement.

Colors

Monochromatic backgrounds (#e8e4e0). No dark colors — only soft whites, creams, and light grays. The background IS the shadow reference.

Typography

Inter (sans-serif) — light weight, clean, unassuming. Low contrast between text and background for that soft look.

Characteristics

Dual shadow (light + dark), same-color-as-background elements, emboss/deboss effect, rounded corners, no borders, monochromatic.
